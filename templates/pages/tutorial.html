Getting Started

Your first steps to luxury fragrances

1

Visit Our Homepage

Start at our homepage to see featured products, new arrivals, and special offers. The homepage showcases our best fragrances.

Featured Products New Arrivals
2

Use Dark/Light Mode

Click the / icon in the top navigation to switch between light and dark modes for comfortable browsing.

3

Navigate the Menu

Use the navigation bar at the top to access different sections:

  • Home - Back to the main page
  • Shop - Browse all products
  • Categories - Shop by perfume type
  • About - Learn about us
  • Contact - Get in touch

Browsing Products

Find your perfect fragrance

1

Search for Products

Click the search icon in the navigation bar to search for specific perfumes by name, brand, or scent notes.

Try Search Now
2

Browse by Category

Visit Categories to browse by perfume type:

Women's Perfumes
Men's Colognes
Unisex Fragrances
Gift Sets
3

Filter & Sort Products

On the shop page, use filters to narrow down your search:

  • Filter by price range
  • Filter by brand
  • Sort by price (low to high / high to low)
  • Sort by newest arrivals
  • Sort by popularity
4

View Product Details

Click on any product to see detailed information including:

  • High-quality product images
  • Full description & scent notes
  • Available sizes
  • Pricing & discounts
  • Customer reviews & ratings
  • Stock availability

Your Account

Manage your profile and preferences

1

Create an Account

Click Sign Up to create a free account. You'll need:

  • Valid email address
  • Your full name
  • Secure password (8+ characters)

Benefits: Save addresses, track orders, write reviews, and get personalized recommendations!

2

Log In to Your Account

Already have an account? Click Log In and enter your email and password.

Tip: Use "Remember Me" to stay logged in on your device.

3

Access Your Dashboard

Once logged in, click your name or the user icon to access your dashboard where you can:

Edit your profile
Manage addresses
View order history
See your wishlist
Change password
Notification settings
4

Forgot Your Password?

No worries! Click Forgot Password on the login page. Enter your email and we'll send you a reset link.

Cart & Wishlist

Save and buy your favorite items

1

Add Items to Cart

On any product page, select your preferred size and click "Add to Cart". You'll see a confirmation and the cart icon will update with the item count.

You can add multiple items and adjust quantities later!

2

View Your Cart

Click the cart icon in the navigation or visit your cart to:

  • See all items you've added
  • Update quantities
  • Remove items you don't want
  • View subtotal and estimated total
3

Save to Wishlist

Not ready to buy? Click the heart icon on any product to save it to your wishlist. Access your wishlist anytime from your dashboard.

Tip: Create an account to keep your wishlist saved across devices!

Checkout Process

Complete your purchase securely

1

Go to Checkout

From your cart, click the "Proceed to Checkout" button to start the checkout process.

2

Enter Shipping Information

Fill in your delivery details:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Street address
  • City, State, and Country

Logged in? Your saved addresses will appear for quick selection!

3

Choose Shipping Method

Select your preferred delivery option:

Standard Delivery

3-5 business days nationwide (₦2,500)

Express Delivery

2 days for Lagos, Benin, Warri (₦5,000)

Store Pickup

Pick up from our store (FREE)

4

Make Payment

We use Paystack for secure payments. After clicking "Place Order", you'll be redirected to Paystack where you can pay using:

Visa Card
Mastercard
Bank Transfer
USSD
Bank App

Your payment is 100% secure and encrypted. We never store your card details.

5

Order Confirmation

After successful payment, you'll see an order confirmation page with:

  • Your unique order number
  • Order summary
  • Confirmation email sent to you
  • Estimated delivery date

Track Your Orders

Stay updated on your delivery

1

View Order History

Log in and go to your Dashboard → Orders to see all your past and current orders with their status.

2

Understand Order Statuses

Your order will go through these stages:

Pending Order received, awaiting payment
Confirmed Payment confirmed
Processing We're preparing your order
Shipped On the way to you!
Delivered Successfully delivered
3

Track Shipment

Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Click on your order to see tracking details and estimated delivery time.
