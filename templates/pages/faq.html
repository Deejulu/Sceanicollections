Orders & Shipping

Delivery within Lagos takes 1-2 business days. For other states in Nigeria, delivery takes 3-5 business days. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Yes! We offer free shipping on orders over ₦50,000. Orders below this amount have a flat shipping fee of ₦2,500 within Lagos and ₦3,500 for other states.

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Products

Absolutely! We guarantee 100% authenticity on all our products. Every fragrance is sourced directly from authorized distributors and comes with original packaging and batch codes.

EDT (Eau de Toilette) contains 5-15% fragrance concentration and typically lasts 4-6 hours. EDP (Eau de Parfum) has 15-20% concentration and lasts 6-8 hours or longer. EDP is generally more expensive but offers better longevity.

Store perfumes in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Avoid storing in bathrooms where humidity fluctuates. Keep the cap tightly closed to prevent evaporation.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused, sealed products in their original packaging. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

If your product arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund immediately.

Payment

We accept card payments (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and pay on delivery (within Lagos only). All online payments are securely processed through Paystack.

Yes! All transactions are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways. We never store your full card details on our servers.
